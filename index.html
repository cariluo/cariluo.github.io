About Us

Our group focuses on developing new methods for electronic structure theory and applying these methods to real-world problems in chemistry and materials science.

Research

We are working on various projects including Bootstrap Embedding, DFT Embedding, and more. Our research aims to improve accuracy and efficiency in computational chemistry.
